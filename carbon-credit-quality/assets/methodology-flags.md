# Methodology flags — known concerns by methodology ID

Not exhaustive. Update as ICVCM CCP-eligibility assessments and methodology revisions land. Always check the registry website for the current version of any methodology before relying on this file.

## Forestry — REDD+ (avoided deforestation)

### VM0048 (Verra) — Reducing Emissions from Deforestation and Forest Degradation
- Replaces VM0006/VM0007/VM0009/VM0015. Anchors project baselines to jurisdictional reference data (national or sub-national).
- Concerns: relatively new; legacy projects transitioning from VM0007 may see significantly different credit volumes post-transition — model the delta before underwriting.
- Companion module: VMD0055 (allocation of jurisdictional baseline to projects).

### VM0007 (Verra, legacy) — REDD+ Methodology Framework
- Project-scale baseline-setting; widely criticised in peer-reviewed literature for systematic over-crediting via short or anomalous reference periods.
- Treat legacy VM0007 issuances with strong scepticism; expect significant value impairment for unretired stockpiles.

### ART-TREES (ART registry) — The REDD+ Environmental Excellence Standard
- Jurisdictional approach (national or large sub-national). Structurally avoids most VM0007-style baseline concerns.
- Aligned with LEAF Coalition demand. Lower per-credit risk; verify the host jurisdiction has independent forest-cover monitoring (e.g., national MRV system tied to UNFCCC reporting).

## Forestry — ARR (afforestation / reforestation / revegetation)

### AR-ACM0003 (CDM legacy, used under VCS) — A/R on lands except wetlands
- Most-used ARR methodology. Concerns: monoculture plantation risk; permanence given fire/pest; leakage if pre-project land was in productive use; species-mix transparency.
- Check: native-species share, fire management plan, tenure documentation.

### VM0047 (Verra) — Afforestation, Reforestation and Revegetation
- Newer ARR methodology with tighter additionality and dynamic performance benchmarks.
- Preferred over AR-ACM0003 for new projects.

## Forestry — IFM (improved forest management)

### VM0012 / VM0010 (Verra) — IFM in temperate and boreal forests
- Concerns: counterfactual harvest schedule is the central judgement; "paper additionality" risk where landowner had no realistic plan to harvest.
- Cross-check with FSC/SFI certification status and harvest plans pre-project.

### ACR IFM (American Carbon Registry) — US-focused
- Generally rigorous; California IFM projects under CARB (compliance, but methodology informs VCM) have been the subject of CarbonPlan critique on common-practice baselines.

## Agriculture — soil carbon / ALM

### VM0042 (Verra) — Improved Agricultural Land Management
- Dominant soil-carbon methodology. Supports model-based, measurement-based, and hybrid quantification.
- Concerns: high MRV cost if measurement-based; model uncertainty if model-based (typically DNDC or DayCent); additionality where regen practices were already spreading regionally; permanence given soil-pool reversibility.
- Check: sampling design (stratified random?), model calibration evidence, uncertainty discount applied, reversal monitoring plan.

### CAR Soil Enrichment Protocol — US grasslands and croplands
- US-specific; well-respected on protocol rigor; smaller volume than VM0042 globally.

## Blue carbon — wetlands

### VM0033 (Verra) — Methodology for Tidal Wetland and Seagrass Restoration
- Key blue-carbon methodology. Concerns: subsurface MRV is hard; tenure and IPLC rights frequently complex; sea-level-rise and storm permanence risk.
- Check: hydrological monitoring frequency, sediment-accretion data, benefit-sharing arrangement.

### VM0007 REDD+MF module for mangroves
- Legacy mangrove projects; treat with same scepticism as other VM0007-family projects.

## Engineered removals — DAC / CCS / BiCRS / enhanced weathering

- Methodologies emerging across registries (Puro.earth, Isometric, Verra DAC, CDM CCS-LFG). Permanence is typically strong (geological storage), MRV is industrial-process measurement.
- Check: lifecycle accounting if capture energy is fossil-derived; monitoring of injected CO2 plume for CCS; verification of feedstock for BiCRS.

## Cookstoves

### Gold Standard TPDDTEC — Technologies and Practices to Displace Decentralised Thermal Energy Consumption
- Concerns: usage-rate assumption is the central driver of credit volume. Older versions of the methodology allowed self-reported usage rates that are now understood to over-credit significantly.
- Check: sensored stove monitoring (SUMs, kitchen performance tests), emission-factor selection, NDC double-counting with host country.

### CDM AMS-II.G — Energy efficiency in thermal applications
- Similar concerns to TPDDTEC.

## Methane

### ACM0001 (CDM, used in VCS) — Flaring or use of landfill gas
- Generally robust on quantification. Additionality: check if host jurisdiction now mandates flaring (e.g., EU Landfill Directive zones, parts of the US under Clean Air Act provisions) — mandated flaring kills additionality.

### Livestock anaerobic digester methodologies (various)
- Generally robust on quantification. Check subsidy stacking with other incentive programs (e.g., RIN / LCFS in the US) — counterfactual changes if other revenues already make the project economic.

## Renewable energy

### ACM0002 and family (CDM, formerly used in VCS / GS / others)
- In most geographies today, grid-connected renewables fail the additionality test (renewables are now standard practice, often the lowest-cost generation source).
- Most major registries have curtailed or excluded grid-RE methodologies. Treat any new issuance with strong scepticism; check host-country LDC status (some methodology windows remain open for least-developed countries) and whether the project would have been economic without carbon revenue.

## How to use this file

1. Identify the methodology ID and version from the project documentation.
2. Look it up here for the dominant concerns.
3. If the methodology isn't listed, search the registry website for the current version of the methodology and the underlying validation/verification report.
4. Don't accept "Verra-verified" or "Gold Standard credit" without a methodology ID — the methodology is the unit of quality assessment.
